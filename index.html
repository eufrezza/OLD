Welcome!

I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at Trinity College Dublin and an Irish Research Council Scholar, under the supervision of Selim Gulesci and Alejandra Ramos. I am also affiliated with the Trinity Impact Evaluation Unit (TIME). My fields of research are at the intersection between Gender and Development Economics.

NEWS: I have been awarded the Luca D’Agliano Research Grant to pursue my research on Intimate Partner Violence and gender norms!

I will be on the 2023-2024 job market.
